Cross-Origin Isolation

TL;DR: Side-channel attacks are not good. Cross-origin isolation can mitigate their impact, making it more difficult for attackers to gain access to a victim origin's data. To opt a document into cross-origin isolation, add the following headers to its response: Cross-Origin-Opener-Policy: same-origin Cross-Origin-Embedder-Policy: require-corp
